## Our Commitment

We believe that a welcoming and respectful environment is essential for fostering collaboration, creativity, and growth. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all contributors, users, and participants involved with the Example Components library.

---

## Our Expectations

You uphold our standards by treating everyone with respect, kindness, and consideration. We expect all interactions — whether through issues, pull requests, discussions, or any other communication channels — to be constructive and inclusive.

---

## Respecting Others

- Be respectful and courteous to all individuals, regardless of their background, experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, ethnicity, or beliefs.
- Use welcoming language and avoid offensive, derogatory, or inflammatory remarks.
- Listen to others’ perspectives and be open to constructive feedback.

---

## Collaboration and Contributions

- When contributing code, ensure your work aligns with the goals and standards of the project.
- Clearly communicate your intentions, ask questions when uncertain, and be receptive to feedback.
- Credit all contributors appropriately and acknowledge diverse perspectives.

---

## Reporting Issues

- If you encounter any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact us via [email](mailto:general@example.com).
- Reports will be handled with confidentiality and respect for privacy.

---

## Enforcement

- We reserve the right to take appropriate action in response to any violations, including warnings, consulting with involved parties, or removal from the project.
- We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or abuse in any form.

---

## Addressing Concerns

If you experience or observe conduct that conflicts with this Code of Conduct, please reach out to us via [email](mailto:general@example.com).

---

*This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open source project standards and is meant to foster an inclusive, respectful environment for everyone involved.*
